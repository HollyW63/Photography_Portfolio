About

I'm Holly — a photographer and sophomore based in Sydney who's fascinated by how people see and interact with the world around them.

I got into photography because it forces me to slow down. There's something about framing a shot that makes you actually look at things — the light on a building, the way a street corner feels at 6pm, the color of clouds before rain.

When I'm not behind a camera, I'm usually reading about cognitive science, building small AI experiments, or thinking about human-computer interaction. The common thread is curiosity about perception: how do we notice things? What makes something feel right?

I shoot on a Panasonic Lumix S9 with a mix of prime and zoom lenses.
